Privacy Policy
Effective Date: February 16, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Balooty ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile Baloot card game application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

Balooty is designed to respect your privacy. We do not require account creation or login to use the App.

Information we do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book data
  • Photos, videos, or files from your device
  • Health or fitness data

Information collected automatically:

  • Device Information: General device type and operating system version, used solely for app compatibility and crash reporting.
  • Game Settings: Your preferences (such as language and sound settings) are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Advertising

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This data may include:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General usage data

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings. You may also remove ads entirely through an in-app purchase.

For more information on how Google handles data in ads, please visit: Google Advertising Policies

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional in-app purchase to remove advertisements. All purchase transactions are processed securely through the Apple App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information.

Data Storage

All game data, statistics, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store user data. If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be deleted.

Children's Privacy

Balooty is a family-friendly card game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). The App does not require any personal information to function. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Apple App Store — for processing in-app purchases (Apple Privacy Policy)

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is typically no personal information to access, modify, or delete. However, you can:

  • Reset your advertising identifier through your device settings
  • Opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
  • Remove ads through the in-app purchase option
  • Uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
